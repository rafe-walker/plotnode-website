Features

Built for how creators
actually work.

Every feature exists because we listened to what's broken in the tools you already use.

🎯

Proportional Visual Timeline

Drag and drop color-coded segments on a timeline that reflects real duration. A 30-second hook looks like 30 seconds. What you see is what you'll film.

🧠

Real-Time Scoring Engine

Every segment change recalculates your score in under 100ms. Move a segment, split a talking head, add B-roll — instantly see the retention impact.

📊

Retention Curve Overlay

Connect YouTube Analytics and see your actual retention curves mapped directly onto your timeline segments. See which segments held viewers and which lost them.

⚡

Energy Curve Design

Set intended energy levels per segment. The engine evaluates your pacing rhythm — pulse pacing between high and low energy keeps viewers watching.

🔮

"What If" Simulation

Drag a segment to a new position and get an instant prediction. Moving this demo earlier could recover 8-12% of viewers at the midpoint.

🔒

Anti-Black-Box Design

Every score shows its reasoning. Every recommendation cites its source. Toggle Advanced Mode to see full formulas. We don't hide anything.

📱

Mobile Shot List

Take your plan on set. Check off shots as you film. Offline-capable PWA — phone in one hand, camera in the other. Status syncs back to desktop.

⌨️

Keyboard-First Design

Cmd+K command palette, full keyboard navigation, The Shredder (Cmd+Shift+Enter to split segments). Built for speed, not clicking around.

🎨

AI Coach Sidebar

Score Ring always visible. Top 3 suggestions with one-tap Apply. Confidence badges on every recommendation. Zen Mode strips it all for pure focus.
