Built For Rural Land

Tools the suburban planners forgot to build.

Every feature is tuned for off-grid, arid Southwest reality. No HOA setbacks. No grid-tied solar assumptions. Just real tools for real dirt.

🛸

Drone-native workflow

Upload OBJ, PLY, glTF, or point clouds from DroneDeploy, Pix4D, OpenDroneMap, RealityCapture, or DJI Terra. Plotnode processes them into streaming 3D Tiles automatically. No CAD knowledge required. Works with any Part 107 operator's deliverables.

🤖

AI co-pilot

Tell it "self-sufficient 10-acre property" and the Auto-Homestead generator drops an optimized starting layout — minimizing earthworks while respecting microclimates, flood zones, and soil suitability.

💧

ADWR wells

See every registered well within a mile of your parcel — depth, casing, water level. No competitor surfaces this.

📜

Zoning warnings

Place a coop within 50ft of an RU-4 boundary? Plotnode flags it before you submit for permit.

☀️

Sun + water physics

Scrub through any date and time to see winter-noon shadows on your solar panels. Rainfall-driven runoff simulation shows exactly where to dig swales.

📱

iOS app with offline AR

Walk your remote parcel without cell signal. The native iOS companion pre-caches your tileset before you leave WiFi, then renders ghosted barns, corrals, and well heads in correct GPS positions through your iPhone camera. ARKit tracking, not janky WebXR.

📋

County PDF exports

Generates permit-grade site plans matching county templates. Cochise RU-4 ALQ format ready at launch; Apache and Mohave next.

🐔

Real rural assets

Pole barns, livestock corrals with squeeze chutes, chicken coops, water tanks, septic systems, ground-mount solar arrays. Procedurally generated — resize and rotate to fit your acreage.

📊

Free off-grid calculators that actually fit your use case

Off-grid solar + battery sizing (load-driven, not roof-driven), water catchment volume, cistern sizing, septic perc estimator, driveway cut/fill, panel tilt for winter optimization, off-grid cabin cost. All free, no signup. Calculator results gate to email for a downloadable PDF.
